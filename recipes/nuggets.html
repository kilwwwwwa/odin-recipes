Chicken Nuggets

Description

Chicken nuggets are a beloved and widely recognized comfort food that has captured the hearts of both young and old alike. These bite-sized delights are typically made from bite-sized pieces of chicken meat that are coated in a crispy and seasoned breading before being fried or baked to perfection. The result is a tantalizing juxtaposition of textures: the outer layer boasts a satisfying crunch, while the interior remains tender and juicy

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bread crumbs
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into nuggets
  2. Mix the bread crumbs, cheese, and seasonings
  3. Dip the nuggets in melted butter, then coat them in the bread crumb mixture
  4. Bake until the chicken is brown and cooked through
