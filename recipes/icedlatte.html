Iced latte

Description

An iced latte is a refreshing and popular coffee beverage that offers a delightful blend of rich espresso and creamy milk, served over ice. This cooling concoction provides a harmonious balance between the robust flavor of coffee and the soothing smoothness of milk. To create an iced latte, a shot or two of espresso is pulled and then combined with chilled milk, typically in equal or customizable proportions, depending on personal taste preferences. The result is a mellow yet invigorating drink, perfect for warm weather or for those who prefer a cooler coffee option

Ingredients

  • 3 espressos
  • 230 ml milk (or 1 coffee cup)
  • 6 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Pour the ice cubes into your blender
  2. Prepare your three espressos
  3. Pour the hot espressos into your blender
  4. Add two teaspoons of sugar for the gourmet side
  5. Pour your preparation into a large glass and add the milk according to your convenience
  6. Serve immediately. ENJOY!
