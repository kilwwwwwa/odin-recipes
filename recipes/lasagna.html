Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has captured the hearts and palates of people around the world. It is a culinary masterpiece that consists of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, typically alternating with rich and savory fillings. One of the most traditional variations features a mixture of ground meat, often beef or pork, simmered in a flavorful tomato sauce. Each layer is complemented by creamy bechamel sauce, which adds a velvety texture and a touch of indulgence to the dish. Generous amounts of grated cheese, commonly mozzarella and Parmesan, are sprinkled between the layers and atop the final layer, creating a golden and gooey crust when baked

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
